Ciudad Rodrigo


On the sixth day of our tour, we ended up making a stop in our final town in Spain. Ciudad Rodrigo is a small town with a population of 12,500 people known for its 12th-century walls. While in town, we had a brief walking tour. Though our time there was short, it was a beautiful way to end our time in Spain. After our walking tour, we went back on the bus to make our way to the first city we would make a stop at in Portugal.
